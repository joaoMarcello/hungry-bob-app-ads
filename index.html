Hungry Bob

About the Game

A cat. Lots of fish. And no time to rest.

In Hungry Bob, you control a hungry — and slightly temperamental — cat in a never-ending fish frenzy. This is a fast-paced 2D arcade platformer where fish fall from the sky and your mission is simple (yet desperate): catch only the fish Bob wants at that moment. Grab the wrong one, or miss it, and you're in trouble.

And it gets worse: pufferfish fall too. Bob can't touch them, can't hit them — just avoid them at all costs. One wrong move and it's game over.

With charming retro pixel art and a genuine CRT filter, Hungry Bob brings back the spirit of '90s arcades in quick, addictive rounds perfect for mobile gaming.

The speed increases, Bob's tastes change constantly, and only the sharpest players will reach the top of the global leaderboard. Got a gamepad nearby? Bob's cool with that too.
